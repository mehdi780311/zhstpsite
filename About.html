About Us

Our Mission

To support and accelerate the growth of knowledge-based companies in the health sector by providing state-of-the-art infrastructure, mentorship, and access to a network of experts. We aim to translate research into impactful commercial products.

Our Vision

To become a leading science and technology park in the region, recognized for its contribution to public health, economic development, and fostering a vibrant entrepreneurial culture.
